Rice Bowl

Description

Vegetarian rice bowl. Also untried. How did you guess?

Ingredients

  • Instant rice
  • Vegetables pouch
  • Egg
  • Soy sauce
  • Butter
  • Sriracha

How to cook

  1. Cook rice
  2. Cook vegetables
  3. Cook egg using a bit of butter
  4. Mix the rice and vegetables in a bowl with the soy sauce
  5. Place the egg on top
  6. Add Sriracha
