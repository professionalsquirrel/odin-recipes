Honey Chicken

Description

It's actually honey garlic chicken. But honey chicken sounds cuter. So I name it as such. Could be good, never tried it.

  • Chicken
  • Garlic powder
  • Garlic cloves
  • Water
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper and garlic power
  2. Heat a pan over medium high, sear chicken until cooked through
  3. Add garlic between the chicken, fry until fragrant
  4. Add honey, water, vinegar and soy sauce
  5. Increase heat to medium-high and continue to cook until sauce reduces down and thickens a little
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve over vegetables rice pasta or with a salad
