Lemon Pasta

Description

This is just pasta with lemon and garlic. Great as a side dish. Never tried it. But I have a good feeling about this dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Herbs
  • Salt
  • Water

How to cook

  1. In a heated pan, add garlic, herbs, lemon zest
  2. Add pasta water
  3. Stir and simmer
  4. Toss in the cooked spaghetti and serve
